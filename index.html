About me / 关于我

A Postdoc researcher from the Department of Materials Science and Engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, China / 哈尔滨工业大学（深圳）材料科学与工程系博士后研究员

A Fan of FCB, LFC, Lakers, and Dota2 / FCB、LFC、湖人队和Dota2的粉丝

I obtained the Bachelor's degree in Materials Science and Engineering from Tianjin University in 2015, the Master's and doctoral degree in Mechanical Engineering from HK PolyU in 2018 and 2021. / 我于2015年在天津大学获得材料科学与工程学士学位，2018年和2021年在香港理工大学获得机械工程硕士和博士学位。

My research interests include high entropy alloys, high-performance steels, and materials informatics. / 我的研究兴趣包括高熵合金、高性能钢和材料信息学。
